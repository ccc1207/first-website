HOW TO PREPARE FOR YOUR FIRST BESPOKE CONSULTATION

Every successful bespoke story begins with a consultation. This first meeting is usually the most impactful. It lays foundations, gives birth to the jewel's fundamental concepts and outlines the steps moving forward. In order to get the most from your bespoke jewellery consultation, you need to prepare a few things.

HERE ARE SOME TIPS

  • Seek Inspiration - Collect some visual examples of designs
  • Establish Your Budget - This part is all about being real with yourself. How much is a feasible sum for you to spend on this piece
  • Your Ideal Timeframe - Think about when you'd like this piece completed. Are you working towards a specific date? We tend to spend about three weeks on the design stage for a bespoke piece so keep that in mind
